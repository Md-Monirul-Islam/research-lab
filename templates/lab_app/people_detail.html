Biography

Dr. Syed Md. Galib is a Professor in the department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at Jashore University of Science and Technology (JUST), Bangladesh. Before joining JUST, he was working as an Associate Professor in the department of Computer Science and Information Technology at Patuakhali Science an Technology University, Bangladesh.
