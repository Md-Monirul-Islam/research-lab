BIOTECHNOLOGY AND GENETIC ENGINEERING
AT A GLANCE

Biotechnology is a series of enabling technologies which uses biological processes, organisms, or systems to produce/manufacture goods, products, and services intended to improve the quality of life. The interdisciplinary nature of this branch of science encompasses a wide range of disciplines towards the study of animals, plants, and microorganisms to harness the activities for wellbeing. ... It is rapidly gaining significance and opportunities for students, faculty members, and graduates who want to explore the new frontiers of science are simply immense. Keeping the pivotal importance of Biotechnology, the government of Bangladesh has prioritized investing in biotechnology education and research. As a part of this initiative, Khulna University took the leading role of introducing the biotechnology program at the tertiary level.
